Taboo

A fun word-guessing party game for friends and family!

Rules of Taboo

Objective

Get your team to guess as many words as possible within the time limit without saying the forbidden words.

Setup

Split into two teams. Decide on a time limit per round (usually 60 seconds). Select a "clue-giver" from the first team to start.

How to Play

  1. The clue-giver takes a card showing the target word at the top and 5 "taboo" (forbidden) words below it.
  2. When the timer starts, the clue-giver must help their team guess the target word WITHOUT using:
    • Any of the taboo words listed on the card
    • Any part or form of the taboo words
    • Any part or form of the target word
    • Gestures or sound effects
    • "Sounds like" or "rhymes with" clues
  3. A member of the opposing team looks over the clue-giver's shoulder to ensure no taboo words are used.
  4. If the clue-giver uses a taboo word, the opposing team's monitor calls "Taboo!" and the card is discarded with no points.
  5. The clue-giver can pass on difficult cards, but this costs a point.

Scoring

Teams earn one point for each correctly guessed word. The team with the most points after all rounds wins!
